The Replacements On Tour

SUNDAY 25 AUGUST 2013 Their first appearance at the Chicago festival takes place in Humboldt Park from Sept. 13-15, with Fall Out Boy and Blink-182 sharing headlining roles, and the lineup includes Brand New, Flag, Dinosaur Jr., Best Coast, Guided by Voices, and more. The Toronto fest will run from Aug. 24-25 at Fort York and the Denver fest Sept. 21-22 at May Farms.
